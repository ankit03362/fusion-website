What is Cuplock Scaffolding System?

Cuplock scaffolding is a modular system widely used in the construction industry due to its unique locking mechanism. It consists of vertical standards and horizontal ledgers connected using a cup and blade system. This design ensures quick assembly and high load-bearing capacity.

The cuplock system eliminates the need for nuts and bolts, making it one of the fastest scaffolding systems to erect and dismantle. It is ideal for both small and large-scale construction projects.
